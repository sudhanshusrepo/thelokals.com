# Terminology

## Actors
- **Customer (Client)**: The end-user searching for and booking services.
- **Service Provider (Pro)**: The professional offering services (e.g., cleaner, plumber).
- **Admin**: Internal Lokals staff managing the platform.

## Entities
- **Service Request**: A job posted by a Customer or a booking intent.
- **Booking**: A confirmed agreement between Customer and Provider.
- **Category**: A classification of services (e.g., Home Cleaning).
- **Service**: A specific offering by a Provider.

## System Components
- **Lokals Platform**: The aggregate of all backend services, shared logic, and configurations.
- **Lokals Client**: The interface (Web or Mobile) exposed to Customers.
- **Lokals Provider**: The interface (Web or Mobile) exposed to Providers.
